The Zombie Preparedness Team provides technical assistance to cities, states, or international partners dealing with a zombie infestation. Zombie Preparedness Team members are on the frontline, helping the Zombie Preparedness Team scientists work to identify the cause and cure of the zombie outbreak, and helping those in affected areas

Determine root cause: help identify the source of the infection/virus/toxin.

Break the cycle: learn how it is transmitted and how readily it is spread, and prevent further cases.
